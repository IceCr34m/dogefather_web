COMMON FAQs

NET vs Gross Tokens?

In order for Tesla Wallet to receive 400 million NET Tokens you must transfer 444,444,444 tokens from your wallet for the lifetime entry.

I sent 400 Million Gross instead of NET?

No worries send another batch of 44,444,444 tokens and send us a snapshot of the extra transaction with TXID. We will combine and consider as one life-time entry.

I sent 440 Million tokens but the net amount reflect is slightly less than 400 Million
e.g.399,990,000?

No worries, any variation of less than 1% due to miscalculation of slippage will not be considered as a variation. Your lifetime entry is done.

I am from a culture where I don’t like number 4?

Thousands of users from Asia are messaging us that 4 is not their preferred number we can make an exception for such holders and accept their bulk entries. This will mean admins having to do manual data processing in such cases but we respect all cultures & will make the extra effort. So if you don’t like number 4, send 888,888,888 tokens and we will register these as two independent lifetime entries.

Where do we see our confirmations?

Your entries are all visible on BSC Scan. Our team will export all data and share the comprehensive list of entries with all holders on a weekly basis. The weekly list will show number of entries by wallet address and txid.

What if I live in a country where Tesla does not accept blockchain payments yet
how will I get my Tesla?

In such cases we will arrange for a delivery of your car in US along with an immediate trade in through one of the re-distributors / dealers. All such trade ins will only happen after your authorized representative in US (friend / relative) has visited for the necessary photo opportunity. There will be a trade-in hit of approx. 15% charged by the dealer in all such cases.

Will all of us have to wait for our Tesla until the price reaches the target of 0.00018?

No. The Tesla Wallet will keep sending out Teslas on the way up. Everytime collection amount will be equivalent to Tesla price in USD equivalent a car will be issued for the draw immediately. This means that our holders will continue to receive Teslas throughout our journey to final target price.

Is this Wallet only going to benefit Diamond Hand Holders?

No. The Tesla Wallet is for all holders. Diamond hand holders will have higher probability in the early draws but in the future the Tesla Wallet aims at rewarding every holder with a life-time entry in the Tesla Wallet.

If I have transferred my Dogefather tokens from my Wallet to another Wallet will that be
included in the 20% maximum selling criterion to classify Diamond Holders?

No. Token Transfer is not considered as Token Sale.
